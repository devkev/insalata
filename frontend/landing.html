Insalata

An online game for connecting up to 8 players with SALAD!!!!!

Instructions:

The people in the houses would love to have some salad, but they have no ingredients. Please help connect them to the salad ingredients so they can make themselves a salad!

How to play:

  1. Each turn, you will get to draw a line between 2 different coloured tiles.
  2. The lines you have to choose from are highlighted - click on the one you want.
  3. You score points every time you connect one of the 5 ingredients (bowl, lettuce, tomato, cucumber, salad dressing) to a house.
  4. Connecting a second copy of an ingredient to a house (does not need to be the same house as the first copy) often scores more points than the first!
  5. When you connect the last instance of a single ingredient to a house, you get a bonus line! (You can draw any one line for free!)
  6. Connecting all 5 types of ingredients to a single house gets you a special bonus (that house can now have a complete salad, thank you!)
  7. People don't just need salad, they also want to see each other. If you connect two houses with the same letter, you also score points!
  8. Don't forget to keep track how many turns are left in the round!

Tips:

Connecting ingredients is cumulative per round, so try and connect them early!

  1. For example, in Round 1 if you connect a lettuce, a tomato, and a dressing, you score (2 + 2 + 4) = 8 points for the round.
  2. In Round 2, if you additionally connect the second lettuce and both cucumbers, your total score for the round is (2 + 2) + 2 + (3 + 4) + 4 = 17 points.
  3. Your score over the two rounds is 8 + 17 = 25 points.

Scoring:

Your final score consists of:

  1. Your score from connecting targets to houses, over 2 rounds.
  2. 5 points for each pair of connected houses with the same letter.
  3. 15 points if you connect all 5 types of ingredients to a single house. (This only applies once per game).
